Good UX: 10 Usability Heuristics

Good UX can be hard to define, because there are no hard and fast rules that define good UX. A user’s experience is, after all, going to be highly dependent on the user, and one user’s tastes and preferences will almost certainly never match up exactly to another’s. Every user is different.
